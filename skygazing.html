Sky-Gazing



Sky-Gazing Sessions


Knowing about anything starts from observing it over a long period of time, keeping records and analysing the changes. The good thing is about this is, much of it remains the same, at least through a lifespan of a human. Many of the discoveries in space were only observations made for a looooong period of time. Basically, we try to show people the hidden beauty of the space by organizing a sky gazing session once every semester. For that we have an 8 inch Newtonian reflecting type telescope with tracking device, which helps to track the celestial body along its motion
