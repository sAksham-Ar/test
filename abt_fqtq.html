what Is "From Quarks To Quasars" (FQTQ)

Each individual of our Society has a thirst for knowledge. Some lean towards the technical aspect of it, while some others show interest towards the theoretical side of things. Therefore, we have a special group of individuals who have given themselves a rather funky name: From Quarks to Quasars, or better known as FQTQ.

“Fqtq is the name for our space discussion group.” We at FQTQ, have made it our mission to know the physics behind everything, from quarks, the smallest particles in nature to quasars, one of the largest objects in space. Wanna know how a star burns, or what exactly happens inside a black hole? Come to us!
